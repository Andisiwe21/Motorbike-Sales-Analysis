# Bike Sales Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes bike sales data to uncover key business insights related to revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and product performance. The dataset was cleaned and transformed in Excel before being visualized in Tableau to create an interactive dashboard.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance through key metrics such as total revenue, profit, year-over-year growth, top-selling products, customer demographics, and geographic sales distribution.

Through this analysis, the project identifies sales trends, high-performing product categories, customer purchasing patterns, and regional sales opportunities to support data-driven business decision-making.

---

## Project Objective

The objective of this project was to clean, analyze, and visualize bike sales data to answer important business questions and generate actionable insights.

Key objectives included:

- Evaluate overall business performance by analyzing total revenue, profit, and year-over-year sales changes.
- Identify top-performing products by examining product categories and quantities sold.
- Understand customer behavior by analyzing sales patterns across age groups and gender.
- Assess regional performance by visualizing sales distribution across different countries.
- Support strategic decision-making by highlighting growth opportunities and areas requiring business attention.
